In one of the most unique launch failures weâ€™ve seen, Astraâ€™s Rocket 3.3 performedÂ a powerslide off the pad, moving sideways and scorching the grass due to the loss of an engine immediately after ignition. Space is hard, and we wish them the best for their next attempt (that rocket is already mostly finished).
