¬∂An unlikely asteroid impact in 2032. A 50+ meter near-Earth object dubbed 2024 YR4 has a 1.6% probability of impacting Earth in 2032. The object was detected by the automated Asteroid Terrestrial-impact Last Alert System (ATLAS) in Chile on December 27th.
